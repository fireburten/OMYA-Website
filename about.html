The Yelling Man Behind the Data

I'm a data scientist with an unhealthy interest in finding the thing everyone else missed and an even unhealthier interest in telling them about it at length.

My work sits at the intersection of data engineering, analytics, and AI — which mostly means I spend my time writing SQL, yelling at poorly-labelled axes, and building tools that make the messy stuff invisible to everyone else.

Outside the terminal, I'm poking at hardware projects, experimenting with LLMs, and maintaining a running list of planes flying too damn low over my house.
