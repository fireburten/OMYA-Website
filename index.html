Welcome to Old Man Yelling At, a site built on the belief that self-reliance is the only way to escape the "as-a-service" trap. My philosophy is simple: if you have to pay someone else to do it, you’ll never stop paying.

This isn't just another tech blog to regurgitate the tech news; it’s a sandbox for my tinkering. After years as a working at global tech companies like Meta, ViaSat, and HP, I’m done waiting for others to build the tools I need or analyze the things that pique my interest.

I’m using my background in Science, an MBA, and a decade of experience in Data Analytics to build projects that accomplish something. Whether it’s hardware hacking, AI-driven automation, or building my own tooling the goal is always the same: either yell at it like an old man or make something awesome.

Stay off my lawn, and let’s build something real.
